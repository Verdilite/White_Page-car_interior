The ceiling includes

Material and colors

Design ceiling A1 is made of Alcantara. Thanks to its soft structure, the material elegantly emphasizes interior details, creating an atmosphere of sophistication and comfort in the cabin. The ceiling is available in three colors.

Air ducts with interior lighting

The new design of the air ducts, combined with decorative lighting that follows the contour of the air duct, will create a unique style in the interior of your car.

Design ceiling ALTURA "Starry sky" with Alcantara trim

The design decision gives the feeling of a real starry sky inside the car. This effect is achieved by specially selected light for each of the more than 2000 LED "stars". The solution is available in several glow options with the addition of galaxies, the Milky Way, passing meteorites and comets. Adjusting the brightness and flicker of the "Starry sky" carried out using the remote control.

Decorative element with interior lighting and Mercedes-Maybach mirrors

The decorative element installed in the ceiling perfectly complements the interior design of the car, and the contour lighting of the element creates an atmosphere of coziness and comfort. Individually illuminated Mercedes-Maybach mirrors are integrated into the element.

Climate control unit

The control unit, located in the ceiling above the rear seats, provides comfortable access to the climate control system with the ability to adjust the climate inside the car without getting up from your seat.
