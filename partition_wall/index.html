Multifunctional partition

Fast-mounted inter-cabin partition separating the driver and passenger compartments.

Choose the option that suits you

  • SOLO
  • SOLO VISION

The price of the partition is from 762.000 rubles.

Installation of a partition - 25.000 rubles.

The partition includes

Glass transparent deaf

An important component of a multifunctional partition is transparent solid glass, which allows you to monitor the traffic situation on the way.

Climate control system

The climate control system is integrated into the partition, allowing passengers to independently regulate the temperature in the cabin

Decorative element

The decorative element, installed in the central part of the partition, perfectly matches the interior design of the car. Available in four finishes.

Watch

The Mercedes-Benz clock, installed in the decorative element of the partition, is an integral part of the interior.

Organizer pocket

An organizer pocket is integrated into the lower part of the divider. It can store documents, magazines and other things that may be required during the trip.

Antibacterial filter

The events of 2020 have taught us to be more attentive to our health. The interior partition kit includes an antibacterial filter that cleans the air in the passenger compartment from bacteria and viruses.

Partition curtains

Fabric curtains allow you to create a more cozy and private atmosphere. Available in three colors and two drive options: electric or mechanical.

Multimedia complex (included in the SOLO VISION package)

The multimedia complex includes a 15.6-inch high-resolution color display monitor. You can connect your device to the monitor to display and view content from your smartphone or tablet. The complex is equipped with a Wi-Fi module, an HDMI connector, an AV input and an audio output. Convenient remote control.

Backlight

Comfortable lighting, built into the decorative element of the partition, creates coziness and sets the mood for the whole trip. It is possible to individually adjust the backlight using one of 16 colors.

USB connectors

Two additional USB sockets allow you to charge mobile devices and connect other accessories.
